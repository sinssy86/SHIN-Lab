SHIN Lab · Lehigh University College of Health Research across S·H·I·N

The lab studies how community-level environments shape sleep and physical activity — and how those behaviors, in turn, shape health and health equity. We combine wearable and multimodal sensing, GIS and spatial epidemiology, and computational methods, and we work with students and community partners so that measurement leads to translation. The four letters of the lab's name are also its four research pillars.
